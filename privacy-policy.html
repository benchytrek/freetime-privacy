Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Freetime ("we", "us", or "our") is a social event planning app developed by Ben Chytrek. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Freetime iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Freetime:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile photo, provided when you sign up.
  • Event data: Events you create, dates, times, and participant lists.
  • Usage data: App interactions and feature usage, collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the app.
  • Device data: Device identifiers and push notification tokens for sending notifications.
  • Camera: If you use the snap feature, we access your camera. Photos are only shared when you explicitly choose to share them.
  • Crash reports: Anonymized crash data collected via Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable event creation, invitations, and RSVP functionality
  • To send push notifications when friends respond to your events
  • To improve app stability and performance
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics, Analytics) – data is stored on servers located in the EU (europe-west3 region). See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers in the EU (Frankfurt, europe-west3). All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Freetime is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
